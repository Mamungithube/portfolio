Water Break

Sports practice plan manager for athletes & coaches

View on GitHub

Project Overview

Water Break is a backend system for a sports practice planning application built at SparkTech Agency. The app targets coaches and athletes who want a structured, digital way to plan, manage, and track their practice sessions.

Think of it as a planner specifically designed for sports teams — coaches can create detailed practice schedules broken into drills, rest intervals, and exercises, while players can view their upcoming sessions, log attendance, and track personal progress over time.

Key Features

Practice Plan Creation

Coaches can create structured practice plans with multiple drills, rest periods, and timed exercises. Plans can be saved as templates and reused for recurring sessions.

Team & Player Management

Coaches manage teams and roster, assign players to specific sessions, and set individual or group-level training targets. Role-based access separates coach and player capabilities.

Drill & Interval Scheduling

Each practice plan contains multiple drills with set duration, intensity level, and rest intervals. Players can see a real-time breakdown of their upcoming session structure.

Attendance & Completion Tracking

Players mark attendance and log session completion. Coaches get aggregated reports on team participation, completion rates, and individual player consistency.

Progress Analytics

Weekly and monthly progress summaries for both players and coaches. Tracks sessions completed, total training hours, and drill performance over time.

Session Reminders

Automated push notifications and email reminders sent to players before upcoming sessions. Coaches can set custom reminder intervals per plan.

How It Works

1

Coach Creates a Practice Plan

Coach logs in and creates a new session — adds drills, sets duration per drill, assigns rest intervals, and picks a date/time.

2

Players Are Notified

All assigned team members receive a reminder notification with full session details — drills, timing, location, and coach notes.

3

Live Session Tracking

During the session, players can check off completed drills in real-time through the mobile app. Progress is synced live.

4

Post-Session Reporting

After the session ends, attendance and completion data is compiled automatically. Coaches review reports from their dashboard.
